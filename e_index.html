About

Situated in the academic city of Boston, Boston Academy Network (BANET) strives to offer academic consulting founded on a holistic understanding of the American educational system ranging from elementary to world-famous universities. Our primary purpose is to help students achieve independence in making meaningful decisions towards valuable experiences through menotring services catered to each student's personality and unique situation. Our teaching staff composed of IVY League students and alumni offer guidance on developing study habits and the "How" of studying through 1:1 online mentoring sessions.
