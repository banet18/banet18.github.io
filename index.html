Our Services

Services

BANET offers six different services suited to best serve your needs. Click on each for further information.

Education Consulting

Various programs targeted at giving effective consultations for academics, college admissions, study abroad, camp/internships, and early childhood education.

Tutoring/Mentoring

Personalized mentoring sessions aimed at improving a student's learning techniques for effective studying to boost overall academic performance and confidence.

Extracurricular Activities

Guidance for a variety of valuable extracurricular activities such as science, math, history, secondary language, sports, music, and/or art.

IVY/Boston College Tour

An unique opportunity to tour IVY league schools / other excellent universities with a 1-1 in-person conversation with a student of the university.

Study Abroad

Detailed information regarding various schools across the greater Boston area and personalized guidance in choosing the right type of school for the student.

Camp/Internship

Information and preparation for prestigious camps and/or internships during vacation or the school year fitted to a student's interests and talents.

Tutoring / Mentoring
  • Sessions
    •
    • Sessions will be conducted as 1:1 mentoring sessions
    • This program meets 4 times a month but can vary per student
  • Mentors
    •
    • The mentors will be selected from IVY league schools and each student will be matched to a mentor based on the student’s needs and interests.
    • The mentors will keep a record of their observations regarding the student and this information will be used during conferences with the student/parents to track their progress
  • Subjects
    •
    • English/Writing, Essay, Math, Science, History
  • Standardized Test Prep
    • After a pre-test, the student will receive a 1:1 consultation session to determine the best route for test prep
    • PSAT (Preliminary SAT)
    • SAT (Scholastic Assessment Test)
    • ACT (American College Testing)
    • SATII (Subject Test)
    • AP (Advanced Placement Test)
    • SSAT (Secondary School Admission Test)
    • TOEFL
    • MCAT (Medical College Admission Test)
    • LSAT (Law School Admission Test)
    • GRE (Graduate Record Examination)
    • GMAT (Graduate Management Admission Test) 
  • Audience
    •
    • Elementary through college students

Ivy/Boston Colleges Tour
  • Tour Description
    • We provide an opportunity to tour IVY league schools as well as other excellent universities
    • Importantly, we provide an opportunity to have in-person conversation times with a student of the university
    • The guides will be students from the respective universities
    • A tour will only be conducted if there is a minimum interest group of 4 students
  • Accomodations
    •
    • We provide housing and transportation (2 people per room. 3 meals per day-Korean, American, Chinese, or Italian)
  • Tour Itineraries
    • 1 nights 2 days: Harvard + MIT, Outlet shopping, Q&A
    • 2 nights 3 days: 2 days + BU, Berklee college of Music or NEC, Boston tour
    • 3 nights 4 days: 3 days + Columbia University and New York tour
  • Audience
    • Middle school, high school, college students. Parents are welcome
Study Abroad
  • Testing
    • We offer information and guidance on potential required testing such as TOEFL or SAT
  • Accomodation
    • For students requiring home-stay, we will connect you with a family that we have hand-selected using rigorous standards
    • We help any student requiring re-location to Boston
    • For those that require short-term home-stay during school vacations, we connect you to one of our home-stay families
  • Audience
    • 4th to 9th grade students
Camp / Internship
  • Camp
    • We provide information regarding various camps for students during their vacation periods depending on their interests and abilities

    • Camp duration: 1 week-6 weeks (depending on the camp)
  • Internship
    • Selected using assessment of ability in their field of interest and/or major
    • Programs for students during their school or vacation periods
  • Audience
    • Camp: elementary, middle, and high school students
    • Internship: college students

Why
