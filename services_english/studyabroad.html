Study Abroad

  • We provide information regarding various schools across the greater Boston area and will provide guidance in choosing the right type of school (private/public, boarding/day student) for students
  • If testing is required, we offer information and guidance on that
  • For students requiring home-stay, we will connect you with a family that we have hand-selected using rigorous standards
  • We help any student requiring re-location to Boston
  • For those that require short-term home-stay during school vacations, we connect you to one of our home-stay families
  • Audience: 4th ~ 9th grades
