Tutoring/Mentoring

  • Sessions will be conducted as 1:1 mentoring sessions
  • This program aims to provide personalized mentoring sessions aimed at improving student confidence and learning techniques for effective studying to boost overall academic performance
  • The mentors will be selected from IVY league schools and each student will be matched to a mentor based on the student’s needs and interests.
  • The mentors will keep a record of their observations regarding the student and this information will be used during conferences with the student/parents to track their progress
  • This program meets 4 times a month
  • After a pre-test, the student will receive a 1:1 consultation session to determine the best route for test prep
  • Audience: elementary through college students
  • Subject Areas: English/Writing, Essay, Math, Science, History
  • Standardized Test Prep:
    • PSAT (Preliminary SAT)
    • SAT (Scholastic Assessment Test)
    • ACT (American College Testing)
    • SATII (Subject Test)
    • AP (Advanced Placement Test)
    • SSAT (Secondary School Admission Test)
    • TOEFL
    • MCAT (Medical College Admission Test)
    • LSAT (Law School Admission Test)
    • GRE (Graduate Record Examination)
    • GMAT (Graduate Management Admission Test)
